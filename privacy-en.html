Privacy Policy

App: Yeoboseyo (여보세요)  |  Operator: SBLee  |  Effective: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Microphone input (for call analysis only, processed in memory)
  • Speech-to-text results (stored on device)
  • Post-call records (outcome, anxiety score, memo, call time, day, duration, situation type)
  • Speech style profile (auto-analyzed after 5+ scripts, stored on device)
  • Advertising identifier (AdMob)

2. Microphone Use

The microphone is used solely to analyze nearby speech during calls and suggest phrases.
Call audio is never recorded or permanently stored.
Only speech-to-text transcription results may be saved locally on your device.

3. Speech Recognition

This app uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework to transcribe speech.
Speech audio may be processed by Apple's servers under Apple's Privacy Policy.
Transcription results are stored locally on your device only.

4. AI Processing (Gemini API)

Some features use the Google Gemini API. When using script generation or post-call AI analysis, your input text, call situation, outcome, and speech style profile may be sent to Google's servers.
Google Privacy Policy

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve ads.
Google AdMob Policy

6. Error Monitoring (Sentry)

We use Sentry to improve app stability. On errors, error details and context (situation type, language setting) are sent to Sentry. No personal data or user input is transmitted.
Sentry Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention

Call records, scripts, and speech style data are retained on your device until you delete the app or the data manually.

8. Data Security

User data is stored locally. API keys are encrypted using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore.
